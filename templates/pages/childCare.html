A church daycare is a type of childcare service that is operated by a church or religious organization. The curriculum of a church daycare may be designed to incorporate religious teachings, values, and traditions in addition to secular education. The church daycare may be operated as a non-profit organization, and the staff may include qualified childcare providers, teachers, and volunteers who are committed to providing quality care and education to the children. The fees charged for the service may be competitive with those of other daycare centers, and financial assistance may be available to families in need.
