Vision

Our vision is to preserve the sacred teachings of Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Christianity, baptize its believers/children in the name of the Father and Son in the name of the Holy Spirit, preach the gospel, and strive to prepare them to repent and accept the flesh and inherit the kingdom of heaven; To see that spiritual and social services are available to all believers. Fostering spiritual growth, unity and love in our community, preparing them to inherit God's light while sharing it with the world.

Mul'ata

Mul’anni keenya barsiisa qulqulluu amantii Kiristaanummaa Ortodoksii Tewaahidoo Itoophiyaa kunuunsuu, amantoota/ijoollee ishee maqaa Abbaa fi Ilmaa maqaa Afuura Qulqulluutiin cuuphuu, wangeela lallabuu, akka isaan gaabbii galanii foon fudhatan qopheessuuf carraaquu dha mootummaa samii dhaaluu; Tajaajilli hafuuraa fi hawaasummaa amantoota hundaaf akka argamu arguuf. Guddina hafuuraa, tokkummaa fi jaalala hawaasa keenya keessatti guddisuu, ifa Waaqayyoo akka dhaalaniif qopheessuun osoo addunyaaf qoodanii.

Our History

Established in 1995, Hamere Noah Kidane Mihret, St. Michael & Abune Teklehimanot Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Cathedral in Las Vegas embodies the enduring legacy of Ethiopian Orthodox tradition. In that pivotal year, a community of devout believers came together, inspired by their unwavering faith and determination to establish a spiritual haven in the bustling city of Las Vegas. With the blessings of the clergy and the support of the faithful, the cathedral was consecrated, becoming a beacon of hope and salvation for generations to come. Since then, it has served as a sanctuary where believers gather to commune with God, uphold the teachings of Orthodox Christianity, and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopia.

Seenaa Keenya

Bara 1995 kan hundeeffame Hamere Noah Kidane Mihret, St. Michael & Abune Teklehimanot Kaatediraalli Ortodoksii Tawaahidoo Itoophiyaa Laas Veegaasitti argamu hambaa bara baraa duudhaa Ortodoksii Itoophiyaa of keessaa qaba. Bara murteessaa sanatti, hawaasni amantoota ciccimoo ta’an, amantii hin sochoonee fi kutannoo isaanii kan hin sochoone magaalaa Laas Veegaas kan sochii guddaa qabdu keessatti bakka hafuuraa hundeessuuf qabaniin kaka’anii walitti dhufan. Eebba abbootii amantaa fi deeggarsa amantootaatiin kaatediraalichi qulqullaa’ee, dhaloota dhufuuf ibsaa abdii fi fayyinaa ta’e. Ergasii amantoonni Waaqayyoo wajjin walitti dhufeenya uumuuf, barsiisa Kiristaanummaa Ortodoksii kabachiisuuf, hambaalee aadaa badhaadhaa Itoophiyaa kunuunsuuf iddoo qulqulluu ta’ee tajaajilaa tureera.
