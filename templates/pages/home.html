About Us

Our History

Established in 1995, Hamere Noah Kidane Mihret, St. Michael & Abune Teklehimanot Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Cathedral in Las Vegas embodies the enduring legacy of Ethiopian Orthodox tradition. In that pivotal year, a community of devout believers came together, inspired by their unwavering faith and determination to establish a spiritual haven in the bustling city of Las Vegas. read more {% comment %} With the blessings of the clergy and the support of the faithful, the cathedral was consecrated, becoming a beacon of hope and salvation for generations to come. Since then, it has served as a sanctuary where believers gather to commune with God, uphold the teachings of Orthodox Christianity, and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopia. {% endcomment %}
